Zero Inbox - Beta
This app is in heavy beta development. The site will be up and down during this time. It will also be in a read-only mode, so no actual changes to your inbox will occur. Still if you stumble across this page in the wild and it happens to let you login. You can get some insights into your Gmail inbox. Zero Inbox is an attachment to for the Gmail inbox. It will give you suggestions for actions that you can select to take on your inbox. You can also create and manage new actions and filters that it has yet to suggest.
