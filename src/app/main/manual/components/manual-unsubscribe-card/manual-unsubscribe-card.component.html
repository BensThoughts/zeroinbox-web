• Open the menu
  • Select Subscriptions
  • Search for the name of an email list
  • Or scroll and browse for the name
  • Press the unsubscribe button
  • Lists that must be unsubscribed to with a link will display a notice that a new tab/window will open
  • Click unsubscribe to open the link
  • You are now automatically unsubscribed
  • Lists that can be automatically unsubscribed from without opening a link will not display the notice
  • Click the unsubscribe button
  • You are now automatically unsubscribed
