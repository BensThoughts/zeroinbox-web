Manual Cleaning up your inbox can be done in a number of different ways. The recommended steps to using this app are as follows.
  1. Login
    • Login and select an account
    • Wait for your inbox to be downloaded to our servers
    • Large inboxes can take up to 4 or 5 minutes
    • Smaller ones may not even show a downloading phase
  2. Stats
    • After the inbox has downloaded
    • Stats can be seen on the homepage
  3. Subscriptions
    • View all of your subscriptions.
    • Unsubscribe from the ones you no longer wish to receive.
  4. Senders
    • View all people that have sent you email by number of messages or size
    • Label the ones you wish to keep around
    • Trash the ones you no longer care about
    • Bulk trash the remaining senders
It is important to unsubscribe from email lists before deciding which senders to label and delete because after a sender is removed from your inbox it will no longer show up in your list of subscriptions.
