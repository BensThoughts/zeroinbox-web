• The first time you ever use the app the contents of your inbox will be downloaded to our servers
  • This process can take 3 or 4 minutes on larger inboxes and may not even show up on very small ones
  • Feel free to explore other parts of the website while this occurs
  • As soon as the inbox is completely downloaded you will be taken to a homepage that displays some statistics about the current state of your inbox
  • Feel free to come back to these stats later as you clean up to see how they have changed in real time
